How to Play

This is a memory card matching game. Flip cards and match all pairs to win!

Game Modes:

  • Basic: No time limit. Play at your pace.
  • Timer: Finish matching all cards before the time runs out.
  • 1v1: Two players take turns. Matching cards earns a point. Highest score wins.

Difficulty Levels:

  • Easy: 4x4 grid (16 cards)
  • Medium: 6x6 grid (36 cards)
  • Hard: 8x8 grid (64 cards)
  • Extreme: 10x10 grid (100 cards)
